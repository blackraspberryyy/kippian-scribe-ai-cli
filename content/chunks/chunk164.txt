Description
Geraldt Dratzel owns Dratzel's Priestly Goods, a general goods store catered to clerics and priests. It is located in Foenyx.

Most Recent
Session 53: The two find a store. They successfully persuade the shopkeeper, Geraldt, to trade Remus' IOU slip for the ivory strips, a harp, a violin, and some strings. The two add 1 sp and a chipped gold from the Aureate Rapier to balance the deal.
Session 53: Adikia and Kiris sign Geraldt's ledger, claiming that their signatures would attract future customers.
Session 55: Kiris heads back to Geraldt's store. All stores are closed, so Kiris knocks on one of the houses close to it. A rotund man shows on the upper floor window. After hearing Kiris' pleading, the man and Geraldt shows himself to Kiris. Geraldt leads Kiris to his store.
Session 55: Geraldt agrees to sell diamond rings worth 750 gp. Geraldt explains that the diamonds in total is worth 500 gp.
Session 55: Kiris negotiates with Geraldt; he gives the shopkeeper 500 gp and his magic citrine stone in exchange for the diamond rings. After the deal, Kiris heads back to the Chapel of Amlin.
