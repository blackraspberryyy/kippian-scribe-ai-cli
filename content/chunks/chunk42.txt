Description
Aetos is Midelia's elven district. Located on top of an island delta, the district is separated by the river on all sides. Lush vegetation, mimicking the Ephelian flora covers the structures. 

Clan Furwish oversees the district. Laurie Furwish is the district's High Council representative.
