Description
Malvin Randall is a noble hailing from Duospathia.

Malvin appears in the Theater Incident as the member who is wearing Duospathia's ring.

While in Suirsya, Noah trained Malvin to be a scout for the United Kippian Military.
Relationships
Malvin Randall is Mitarella's son and Michaela's half-brother.
